WMPlayer

Responsive music player for your website

WMPlyer is a free, open source web music player.

Features:

  • Full player control - Play, pause, stop, next, previous, fast forward, rewind, seek, volume and loop mode
  • Dynamic playlist - Make your own playlist without any problem
  • Customizable templates - Use one of available templates or create your own
  • Easy to deploy - Dock player wherever you want on site
  • Setup wizard - Create your own WMPlayer with ease
